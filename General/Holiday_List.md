# Holidays

We take 2 week-long breaks each year for summer and winter holidays.

During these breaks the offices are closed.

- 2017-06-12 - 2017-06-16
- 2017-12-25 - 2017-12-31

- 2020-06-22 - 2020-06-28
- 2020-12-21 - 2020-12-27
